# Drawing with Machines: Fall 2025

This page presents projects produced in Golan Levin's Fall 2025 *Drawing with Machines* course at Carnegie Mellon University. In January 2026, these works were presented in an exhibition at the Bantam Tools Machine Arts Gallery in Peekskill, New York.

* Additional and higher-resolution images can also be found in [this Flickr album](https://flickr.com/photos/golanlevin/albums/72177720330070347/).
* Here is the [Fall 2025 Syllabus](../../syllabus/60-428_syllabus_fall_2025.md) and the [Fall 2025 daily calendar](../../lectures/2025/README.md).
